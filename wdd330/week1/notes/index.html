Header Menu & Navigation On Mobile

Master Mobile UX Part 3

In this course, he talks about reachability and how when people are on their mobile device, either one handed or two, it is harder for them to reach the top navigation menu where most websites have it. I've never really thought about that fact, from now on, when I am making a responsive website, I will make sure that in the mobile screen the "top" header is at the bottom to be easily reachable on the phone.
